How FoodBridge Works

Step 1: Sign Up

Restaurants and NGOs create an account to join FoodBridge.

Step 2: List Surplus Food

Restaurants list extra food that is available for donation.

Step 3: NGOs Request Food

NGOs browse the listings and request food as needed.

Step 4: Pickup & Delivery

Food is picked up by the NGO or delivered by a volunteer.
